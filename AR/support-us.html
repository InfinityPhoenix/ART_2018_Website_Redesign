Donate/Sponsor

We are very appreciative of any small or large amount that you can donate! We also have a wishlist with many miscellaneous and not necessarily engineering-related items on it. The names or logos of sponsors and donors are put onto our website and team gear, especially our team shirts. We use donations to buy parts for our robots, register for competitions, and other Aragon Robotics team and club activities.
